Binbin Li   厉 斌斌
Assistant Professor, Yanshan University

I am an assistant professor in the School of Economics and Management, School of Information Science and Engineering, the Yanshan University, Qinhuangdao, China. I am also a member of the Network Sensing and Mobile Computing Group, working with Prof. Wenyuan Liu and Lin Wang.



RESEARCH: RFID, Internet of Things (IoT), and Mobile Computing.
  • RFID Protocols: Identification, Estimation, Polling, Anomaly Detection, Authentication
  • Internet of Things: WiFi Sensing, RFID Sensing
  • Mobile Computing: Smartphone, Smart Home, Activity recognition
